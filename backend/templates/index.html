Welcome to Tuunganes

Based in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Tuunganes is investing in the transformation of society, bringing comfort to vulnerable people and smiles to thousands of families traumatized by war.

Learn More

Faced with persistent insecurity in the eastern Democratic Republic of Congo, Tuunganes is committed to providing long-term solutions by equipping young people and the vulnerable, in order to promote their autonomy. We work to help and support vulnerable populations, while actively contributing to changing behaviors, attitudes, and encouraging free action in the general interest. Our initiative also includes educational support for the most deprived, aimed at providing them with the necessary tools so that they can eventually become self-sufficient.

Mission

Our mission is to make education accessible to all by raising society's awareness of environmental, digital, and legal issues. We promote a culture of interdisciplinary action research to encourage innovation and in-depth understanding of these fields, aiming to create a landscape where every population segment benefits from equal opportunities.

Vision

Our vision is to create a society where education is accessible to all, raising collective awareness about environmental, digital, and justice challenges. We aspire to establish a culture of interdisciplinary action research on the environment and technology, thereby promoting innovation and in-depth understanding of these emerging fields. From this perspective, we envision a landscape where all social strata have access to the same opportunities.
