Welcome to Tuunganes

Based in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Tuunganes is investing in the transformation of society, bringing comfort to vulnerable people and bringing smiles back to thousands of children and families traumatized by war. We take a different approach by sharing our knowledge so that all can benefit, helping to create a society where opportunities are fairly shared

Learn More

Mission

Our mission is to make education accessible to all, by raising society's awareness of environmental, digital, and legal issues. We promote a culture of interdisciplinary action research to encourage innovation and in-depth understanding of these fields, aiming to create a landscape where every population segment benefits from equal opportunities.

Vision

Our vision at Tuunganes is to create a society where education is accessible to all, raising collective awareness about environmental, digital, and justice challenges. We aspire to establish a culture of interdisciplinary action research on the environment and technology, thereby promoting innovation and an in-depth understanding of these emerging fields. From this perspective, we envision a landscape where people and all social strata of the population have access to the same opportunities.
